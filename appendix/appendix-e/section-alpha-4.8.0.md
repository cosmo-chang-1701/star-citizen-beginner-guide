<!-- **DEFENSECON ATTENDANCE EXPECTED TO EXCEED INVICTUS** -->

**DefenseCon 出席人數預期將超越 Invictus**

<!-- **A SURGE OF VISITORS ARRIVED IN THE STANTON SYSTEM AHEAD OF THE EXPANDED EVENT** -->

**隨著活動規模擴大，大量訪客在盛會開幕前湧入史丹頓 (Stanton) 星系**

<!-- After the Navy's unexpected cancellation of their planned Invictus Launch Week in the Stanton system to focus on one central event in the Sol system, Drake Interplanetary quickly seized on the opportunity to expand their annual DefenseCon event. -->

在海軍出人意料地取消了原定於史丹頓星系舉行的 Invictus Launch Week（轉而集中在太陽系舉辦單一大型活動）後，德拉克工業（Drake Interplanetary）迅速把握機會，擴張了其年度 DefenseCon 活動的規模。

<!-- Billed as a celebration of the "Everyday Heroes" of the Empire, the ship manufacturer surprisingly honored the reservations made by their competitors who had already booked space for Invictus and invited them to show off their latest vehicles at the Bevic Convention Center in Area18, ArcCorp. Now it seems that Drake's gamble is going to pay off as the attendance numbers are expected to be significantly higher than those of last year's Invictus. -->

這場被宣傳為慶祝帝國「日常英雄」的盛會中，這家飛船製造商令人驚訝地保留了競爭對手原先為 Invictus 預訂的展位，並邀請他們在 ArcCorp 區 Area18 的 Bevic 會展中心展示最新的載具。現在看來德拉克的這場豪賭即將獲得回報，預計出席人數將顯著高於去年的 Invictus。

<!-- When asked for comment, Drake CEO Anden Arden said, "After years of the same old military pomp, it's no wonder people are ready for a change. Don't get me wrong, this is still a celebration of the Empire. We're just making sure to honor everyone who has an important part to play." -->

當被要求置評時，德拉克執行長 Anden Arden 表示：「在經歷了多年一成不變的軍事壯觀場面後，難怪人們已經準備好迎接改變。別誤會，這依然是對帝國的慶祝，我們只是確保向每一位扮演重要角色的人致敬。」
